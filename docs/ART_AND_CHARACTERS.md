## Art Style Guide

All prompts below are written for a consistent visual identity. Append the following **style suffix** to any prompt for cohesion:

> **Style Suffix:** `fantasy RPG illustration, rich color palette, painterly style with soft lighting, magical glow effects, wizard school aesthetic, semi-realistic digital art`

For pixel-art or retro variants, replace the suffix with:

> **Pixel Suffix:** `16-bit pixel art, fantasy RPG sprite style, vibrant palette, clean outlines`

## Player Character Archetypes

The game has no fixed main character — players create their own wizard. Below are four archetypes that represent different play styles and can serve as promotional art and default avatars.

### 1. The Flame Scholar

**Background:** A passionate young wizard obsessed with the destructive elegance of fire magic. Grew up in a blacksmith's village where they accidentally set the forge ablaze — not with bellows, but with a sneeze. Enrolled at the academy to learn control, but secretly hopes to master the legendary spell *Inferno Tempest*.

**Visual Description:** Warm copper skin, short fiery-red hair with ember-like highlights, amber eyes. Wears a deep crimson robe with gold thread embroidery depicting phoenixes. A leather bandolier holds vials of fire salts across the chest.

**Prompt:**
```
Portrait of a young wizard student with copper skin and short fiery-red hair with glowing ember highlights, intense amber eyes, wearing a deep crimson robe with gold phoenix embroidery, leather bandolier with glass vials of orange fire salts across the chest, warm magical glow around the hands, academy hallway background with floating candles, fantasy RPG illustration, rich color palette, painterly style with soft lighting, magical glow effects, wizard school aesthetic, semi-realistic digital art
```

### 2. The Frost Sage

**Background:** A quiet, contemplative student from the frozen northern highlands. They arrived at the academy with an unusual talent — flowers freeze mid-bloom wherever they walk when emotional. Known for spending hours in the library studying ancient ice runes, they seek the secret to *Eternal Winter*, a spell lost to time.

**Visual Description:** Pale blue-tinted skin, long silver-white hair braided with tiny ice crystals, piercing pale blue eyes. Wears layered robes of deep navy and frost-white, with a fur-lined collar. Frost creeps up from the hem of their robes.

**Prompt:**
```
Portrait of a young wizard student with pale blue-tinted skin and long silver-white braided hair with tiny ice crystals woven in, piercing pale blue eyes, wearing layered navy and frost-white robes with a fur-lined collar, frost creeping up from the hem, snowflakes suspended in the air around them, library background with towering bookshelves, fantasy RPG illustration, rich color palette, painterly style with soft lighting, magical glow effects, wizard school aesthetic, semi-realistic digital art
```

### 3. The Storm Caller

**Background:** A bold, athletic student who grew up on a coastal cliff-side village battered by storms. While other children hid from lightning, this one danced in it. Their raw affinity for lightning magic is unmatched, but wild and unpredictable. They joined the academy not for knowledge, but for the thrill of the Spell Duel Arena.

**Visual Description:** Dark brown skin, short-cropped black hair with electric-blue streak, bright yellow-gold eyes that spark with static. Wears a slate-grey fitted robe with jagged lightning bolt patterns in electric blue, fingerless gloves crackling with tiny arcs of electricity.

**Prompt:**
```
Portrait of a young wizard student with dark brown skin and short-cropped black hair with an electric-blue streak, bright yellow-gold eyes with visible static electricity, wearing a fitted slate-grey robe with electric-blue lightning bolt patterns, fingerless gloves with tiny arcs of crackling electricity between the fingers, stormy sky visible through a tall window behind them, fantasy RPG illustration, rich color palette, painterly style with soft lighting, magical glow effects, wizard school aesthetic, semi-realistic digital art
```

### 4. The Earth Warden

**Background:** Raised in a druid commune deep within an ancient forest, this student has an innate bond with the natural world. They can feel the heartbeat of the earth through their bare feet. Reluctantly sent to the academy by their elders to learn formal magic, they struggle with rigid classroom structure but excel at anything involving living things — potions, creature taming, and earth magic.

**Visual Description:** Olive skin with faint vine-like markings on the forearms, curly dark green-brown hair woven with small leaves and twigs, deep earthy brown eyes. Wears earth-toned robes of mossy green and bark brown, with a living ivy belt that slowly grows.

**Prompt:**
```
Portrait of a young wizard student with olive skin and faint green vine-like markings on the forearms, curly dark green-brown hair woven with small leaves and tiny twigs, deep earthy brown eyes, wearing mossy green and bark-brown layered robes with a living ivy vine as a belt, small glowing mushrooms growing on one shoulder, greenhouse background with magical plants, fantasy RPG illustration, rich color palette, painterly style with soft lighting, magical glow effects, wizard school aesthetic, semi-realistic digital art
```

### 5. The Arcane Prodigy

**Background:** A mysterious transfer student who appeared one semester with no records and a strange marking on their palm — a faintly glowing arcane sigil. They speak little about their past but possess an unusual mastery of raw arcane energy, the rarest and most volatile of all elements. Rumors swirl that they may be descended from one of the academy's founding archmages.

**Visual Description:** Light skin with an otherworldly shimmer, medium-length dark purple hair that seems to shift like a nebula, violet eyes with a faint inner glow. Wears midnight purple robes with shifting constellation patterns, a silver circlet on the brow with a small levitating amethyst.
